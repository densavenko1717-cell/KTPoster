Privacy Policy — KTPoster

Effective date: June 19, 2026

1. Overview

KTPoster is an internal automation tool used to publish content (text, images, and videos) to social media platforms — including TikTok — on behalf of accounts owned and managed by the developer. KTPoster is not a public-facing application and does not collect data from third-party users.

2. Information We Process

KTPoster only processes:

  • Account credentials and access tokens for social media accounts owned by the developer, obtained through official platform APIs.
  • Content (text, images, videos) prepared by the developer for publishing.

KTPoster does not collect, store, or share personal data belonging to third-party users, followers, or visitors.

3. How Information Is Used

Access tokens and data obtained through platform APIs are used solely to:

  • Publish content to the connected accounts.
  • Retrieve the publishing status (success or failure) of each post.

4. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on the developer's own server in a secured manner and are not shared with any third party. Tokens are refreshed or deleted in accordance with each platform's policies.

5. Data Sharing

KTPoster does not share any data with third parties, advertising networks, or analytics services.

6. Data Removal

Account owners may revoke KTPoster's access at any time through their respective platform's app settings (e.g., TikTok > Settings > Manage apps, or Instagram > Settings > Apps and Websites). For any other data removal requests, contact the email below.
